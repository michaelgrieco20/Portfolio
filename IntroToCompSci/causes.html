World Hunger

World Hunger is destroying the youth of the world, and will destroy our future.

World Hunger has been stunting the growth of hundreds of millions of children, mostly in third world and developing nations. The majority of those children live in Asia and Africa1.

Hungry children are dying every day from malnutrition and other diseases that are obtained by not eating properly. The scarcity of nutrients and vitamins that these children consume is depriving them of the ability to have a proper life.
